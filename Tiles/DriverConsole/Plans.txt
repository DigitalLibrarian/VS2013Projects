Goal: To build an interesting tile engine on par with dwarf fortress or cataclysm dda.  

Most things in the simulation should be completely data driven.  The list includes:

* characters
* weapon combat moves and capabilities
* all inventory items
* body
* atlas data (terrain / world info)
* world generation parameters
* civilizations
* materials
* structures
* vehicles
* biomes
* sites

* religions
* world history
* character psychological model (dreams, wants, needs, relationships)
